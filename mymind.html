My mind, My world and my Life

My name is Angel Nerozzi, for today’s date I am a young man of 20 years old, I am passionate about many things the truth, but I have my favorites.

I like to think that there is always a solution for everything, even for the biggest adversities, I like to believe that technology together with the business world of disruptive entrepreneurship can make the world a better place.

If I could describe myself in one sentence it would be something like: An eager every day to discover, investigate, learn and know something new.

In this blog, I plan to write all the thoughts I may have that involve learning every day from the best companies, the development of new technologies and anything that involves adventure or risk at all.

Welcome to my world, my greatest satisfaction can be knowing that as I do this, you will enjoy every research, thought and everything that can be published here. Thank you very much for your attention.
